Research intern, Samsung

This project is with Samsung R&D India that deals to develop an Active Speech Recognition (ASR) model for identifying filler words in conversations. Filler words are non-content words that are used to fill pauses in speech, such as "um", "uh", and "like". They can be difficult for ASR models to recognize, as they are not part of the vocabulary that the models are trained on. The goal of this project was to develop an ASR model that can accurately identify filler words in conversations. This will allow the model to better understand the content of the conversation, and it can also be used for confidence analysis and other tasks.
